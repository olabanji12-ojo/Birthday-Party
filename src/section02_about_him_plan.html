Animation Plan — Scroll Triggered
1
Section label + heading slides in from left
The "About the Celebrant" label and the big name heading slide in from x:-40 to x:0, fading in. The nickname "Big Daddy" follows 0.15s after with the same motion.
Trigger: whileInView · initial: x:-40, opacity:0 · animate: x:0, opacity:1 · duration: 0.6s
2
Gold divider line draws itself
The ornamental gold divider line between the heading and description animates its width from 0 to full — like it's being drawn across the page. Elegant and satisfying.
Trigger: after heading · initial: scaleX:0, originX: left · animate: scaleX:1 · duration: 0.8s · easing: ease-out
3
Description text fades up
The body description paragraphs fade in and drift up (y:15→0). Then the blockquote follows with a 0.2s delay, sliding in from slightly below.
Trigger: whileInView · initial: y:15, opacity:0 · animate: y:0, opacity:1 · duration: 0.5s · delay: 0.2s after divider
4
Bento photos cascade in from right
The three bento photos animate in one by one — Photo 1 (large left) appears first, then Photo 2 (top right), then Photo 3 (bottom right). Each slides in from x:40→0 and fades in. Stagger of 0.15s between each photo.
Trigger: whileInView · initial: x:40, opacity:0 · animate: x:0, opacity:1 · stagger: 0.15s · duration: 0.5s each
5
Photo hover interactions
Each bento photo on hover: scale 1→1.04 (zoom in slightly) + gold border glows (box-shadow: 0 0 0 2px #c9a84c). Smooth transition 0.3s. On mobile: no hover — just static with gold borders.
Framer Motion whileHover · scale: 1.04 · transition: 0.3s ease

Animation Plan — Scroll Triggered
1
Section label + heading fades up from center
"Event Details" label and the section heading both fade in and drift up from y:20→0. Centered text, feels like the section is waking up.
whileInView · y:20→0, opacity:0→1 · duration:0.6s · easing:easeOut
2
3 cards stagger in left to right
Card 1 (Date) slides up first, then Card 2 (Time), then Card 3 (Venue). Each card: y:40→0, opacity:0→1. 0.15s stagger between each card. Feels like they're being dealt onto the table one by one.
staggerChildren:0.15s · each card: y:40→0, opacity:0→1 · duration:0.5s · spring easing
3
Card icons pulse once on entry
After each card appears, its icon does a single quick scale pulse — scale 1→1.2→1 over 0.3s. Like each card is "announcing" itself. Subtle but adds life.
Framer Motion onAnimationComplete · scale:1→1.2→1 · duration:0.3s · once only
4
Map slides up and fades in
The map iframe and directions button slide up together (y:30→0, opacity:0→1) after the cards have appeared. Delay of 0.5s after cards finish so it feels like a natural continuation.
whileInView · y:30→0, opacity:0→1 · duration:0.6s · delay:0.5s after cards
5
Card hover — gold glow lift
On hover each card lifts slightly (y:-4) and the gold border brightens (border-color: #c9a84c full opacity). Gives a premium "interactive card" feel without being distracting.
whileHover · y:-4 · border brightens · transition:0.25s ease
